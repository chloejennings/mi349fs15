What is that?

Given the choice between functionally equivalent designs, the simplest design should be selected.

How do I use it when designing?

Let's say Clara, a cake shop owner, wants me to design a simple blog post that outlines how beautiful her cakes are so people will order them. I asked two people to design a simple post, let's decided which one accomplishes our goal the most.

Although this image is decortative and showcases Clara's cakes it does not have a feature that allows the user to buy here cakes.
This design showcases not only one cake but 4! It also has a link to Clara's online ordering site where the user can buy whatever cake they need! Since this blog post is more functional to what Clara needs this is the one we will choose.
