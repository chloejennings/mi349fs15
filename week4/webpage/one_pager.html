Chloe Jennings One Pager

Short Pitch

A well thought out design to showcase Chloe’s professional method using interactivity followed by her designs and attributes.

Long Pitch

Cjennings.com will open up with an interactive visualization of methods and steps used by Chloe to create a finished design. The user will then be introduced to the home screen where they have the option to view the interactive visualization again, see more finished designs by Chloe, or learn more about her and connect via social media. This site will be used by Chloe as a digital portfolio to showcase her knowledge on interactivity and design to potentially assist in her recieving her dream career.
