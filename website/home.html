There is one thing I want you to know about me: my opinion as to whether design is considered art.


Starting out college as a Communications major I learned that how the user interacts with your work is the main competent to any design. After switching my major to Studio Art: Graphic Design I was given the opportunity of a greater freedom to be elaborate with my designs, making them art. While the second studio art method of design might work better given a certain project but I whole heartily believe that design is about the user and they interact with it. So no I do not consider my designs to be artwork, I consider them to be the best solutions for the problem that was give to me.
